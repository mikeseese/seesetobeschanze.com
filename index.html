About Us

Almost four years to the exact day we first met in Gainesville during summer term, we’re tying the knot! After a stereotypically swampy day found Kirsten reading in her hammock outside, Mike quickly took notice. What started off as a summer fling with the girl next-door (or in this case, downstairs) quickly turned into years of adventures, heated intellectual debates, and binge-watching Netflix. We’ve managed to survive it all, from four months of living on separate continents to first real jobs and graduate school all-nighters. And after a grand geocaching proposal, we cannot wait to begin our next adventure with you all by our sides!

Our Story

Michael is more than I could have ever asked for. I may be a tad biased, but I think he is extremely smart, sincere, witty, and sensitive, once you get past that cocky exterior of course. Falling in love with him was easier than I would have liked; we simply just clicked. I quickly found myself head-over-heals with the quirky engineer-hermit from upstairs. Every day with Michael is an adventure. His passion, whether it be for proving me wrong in a debate or for developing world-changing technologies, still blows me away every day. On bad days, he is the person I know I can turn to when I need advice or a good ugly cry; and on good days, he’s my biggest cheerleader and always willing to grab a coffee or a celebratory drink. I still don’t know how he puts up with me and my snarky self, but I truly don’t know what I would do without him. I can’t wait to officially call you mine!

Kirsten is funny, brilliant, and most wonderful person I know. When I first met Kirsten, she had an adorable, dorky-looking Princess Leia hair-do. She was setting up her hammock outside of my apartment building to read. What a nerd :). However, I found myself making the double-take...then the triple-take..then the trash-take (taking out the trash to introduce myself). Kirsten's perseverance and hard work to overcome unexpected obstacles continues to impress me every day. Kirsten knows me more than anyone else. She understands my habits, she knows when I'm upset, and she knows how to make me perpetually happy. I do not know what I would do without having my best friend and soulmate by my side for the rest of our lives. I am excited to stand next to her as we recite our vows!
