Ceremony

4PM Lake Ashby Farm

Our marriage ceremony will take place at Lake Ashby Farms in New Smyrna Beach, FL. Onsite parking is free and there will be a shuttle to the site for those who cannot make the short walk over to the location. The ceremony will take place outside in the lawn in front of a giant oak tree. In the event of rain, we will move to the adjacent barn and try to keep dry!
